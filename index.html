Sophia,

You are the most precious treasure in my life. Thank you for always being there—for me and for our girls. We love you deeply, and I hope that as you look through these photos, they bring back the joy of all the beautiful moments we’ve shared.

My love for you.

Thank you for always being my partner, my heart, and my home.

Gaspar Dantas
